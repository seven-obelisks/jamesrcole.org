About

jamesrcole.org is the online homepage of James Cole, a communications and public relations professional based in Central Illinois.

I’m currently part of the Illinois Council on Problem Gambling, where I help spread awareness about problem gambling and connect healthcare professionals with treatment resources.

Here, you’ll find my professional resume, digital portfolio, as well as selections of my film photography.

how it’s built

This website is built with Hugo, an open-source static site generator, and hosted on Cloudflare Pages.

Like many of my projects, this site is designed to be as lite and accessible as possible.

More information is available on the site’s GitHub page. The entirety of this site’s architecture is made available under the MIT License.
